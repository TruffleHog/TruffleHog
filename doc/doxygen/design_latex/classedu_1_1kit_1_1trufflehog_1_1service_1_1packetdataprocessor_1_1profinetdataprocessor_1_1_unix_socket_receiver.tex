Otherwise the service starts receiving packet data from the spp\+\_\+profinet snort plugin, packs the data into \hyperlink{classedu_1_1kit_1_1trufflehog_1_1service_1_1packetdataprocessor_1_1profinetdataprocessor_1_1_truffle}{Truffle} objects and then generates \hyperlink{}{I\+Truffle\+Command} objects and sends them to all listeners.
